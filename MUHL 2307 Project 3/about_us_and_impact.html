About the Festival and Its Impact

About Us:

Everyone is welcome in the Chromatic Harmony Festival! In this event, every one not only has the chance to experience music from around the world, but also the music genres produced through cultural fusions. Artists from different parts of the world will gather at this event to express themselves and display their music that was born from the unity between multiple cultures. In music, different multiple genres are represented by different colors. For example, pop can be represented with the color pink, while jazz and blues are represented by the color blue. However when we combine these different genres or “colors,” we are able to create new and sometimes beautiful shades of colors, hence the name of the festival. Another reason why this festival is created is that it not only allows artists to display their music inspired from multiple cultures, but it also allows some artists to spread their message and encourage change in their countries.. Some of these artists come from countries that are currently in conflict, whether it is caused by war, poverty, or discrimination. While they are representing their genre of music in this festival, it also gives the opportunity for these artists to spread their message to raise awareness to these issues and encourage change to improve not only their lives, but also the lives of others who are suffering through something similar.

Impact:

The purpose of this festival is to highlight the impact of globalization through migration and the technology to the current day. For most of history, most of globalization has happened due to immigration either through forced migration due to conflict or through people who are currently trying to find a better life outside of their home country. For example, several European countries such as Great Britain and Spain both had influence in the development of \countries around the world. Even after they are no longer influenced by other countries, these cultures are still evolving by having other immigrants either voluntary or non-voluntary, like slavery. However, ever since the rise of technology such as radio, tv, and even the internet has facilitated globalization, by allowing people to experience different cultures without having to leave the comfort of their home. This type of globalization has allowed for different cultures to integrate a lot quicker than the last thousand years. However, sometimes globalization has led to negative effects, as some cases have led to the cultures being suppressed like Israel and Palestine, to which other parts were used to try to counter this, like Palestinian hip hop. However different parts of globalization have also changed to positive changes, such as giving others a sort of a lifeline when they feel like they are isolated in their home country alone. Overall, the changes and impacts caused by all of this are highlighted in this festival. The artists selected for this festival represent the different aspects and effects of globalization. While some of these artists have been directly affected or benefitted from the effects, other artists have also taken advantage of globalization as well to create something new and beautiful. This in turn, will create new aspects of each of their cultures contributing to globalization. In this festival we want to bring awareness to the changes that appeared as a result of this phenomenon. Throughout this festival, we also hope to encourage others to notice the effects of globalization present in their own culture and bring some appreciation to some of the changes, no matter if they are positive or negative.
